Cost Distribution

Frame Construction

  • Wood: Kiln-dried hardwood for arms, legs, and base rails
  • Plywood: Seat and back panels
  • Joinery: Mortise-and-tenon joints reinforced with screws and glue
  • Support: Elastic webbing and zigzag springs for seat suspension

Cushioning

  • Seat Foam: 6″ high-density (30–35 density)
  • Back Foam: 4″ medium-density (25–28 density)
  • Fiber Fill: Polyester batting for softness and shape retention

Upholstery

  • Material: Genuine leather, double-stitched seams
  • Padding: Foam wrapped with batting before leather application
  • Finishing: Hidden zippers or Velcro for removable cushions
